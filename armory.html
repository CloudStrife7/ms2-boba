The Journey

BUILD LOG: BH-7024
Phase 01 — January 2024

The Decision

After fifteen years in the 501st Legion and two completed builds — a Biker Scout and a Galactic Marine — it was time for the ultimate challenge. Boba Fett had always been the dream. Not just any Fett, but the Re-Armored version from The Mandalorian Season 2. The battle-scarred legend who reclaimed his father's legacy.

The parts list was drawn up, vendors were contacted, and the mission was officially a go. As Squad Leader of Devastator Squad, there was a standard to uphold. This build had to be screen-accurate, and it had to be done right.

Phase 02 — May 2024

Parts Arrive

The BobaMAker armor set arrived first — and it was immediately clear this was a cut above. Finished edges, clean pulls, ready for paint with almost no modification needed. Out of every armor kit purchased over fifteen years of building, this was the best.

Then the RedBowProps V3 helmet showed up. Pre-assembled, painted, and weathered to perfection. Holding it for the first time — officially the coolest Star Wars item in the collection. The build was becoming real.

Phase 03 — June–September 2024

Paint & Weather

This is where the magic happens. An armor party was organized — friends, spray cans, and a shared mission to get that screen-accurate weathering. The bondo and acetone method was new territory, but the results spoke for themselves. Stippling, dry brushing, acetone washes — each technique layered on top of the last.

Montana Black spray paint became the backbone — applied with flat jet caps for that authentic, worn-in finish. The raw armor was transforming into something that looked like it had survived a sarlacc pit.

Phase 04 — September–October 2024

Assembly

With all the pieces painted and weathered, the assembly phase began. Velcro was attached to every armor plate. Decals were carefully applied. Black washes brought out the panel lines and added depth to every surface. The gauntlet hoses were routed, the knee darts were mounted, and the jet pack harness was fitted.

Each component that went on brought the costume closer to life. What had been a collection of individual parts was becoming Boba Fett — piece by piece, detail by detail.

Phase 05 — October 2024

First Deployment

Boba Fett Lives. The first full test run happened at Boo at the Zoo — surrounded by friends, fellow troopers, and hundreds of Star Wars fans who had no idea they were looking at months of obsessive work. The compliments poured in. Kids pointed and stared. Parents asked for photos.

Everything fit. Everything worked. The armor moved the way it should, the jet pack sat right, and the helmet visor framed the world exactly like it was supposed to. It was a huge success — and proof that the mission was nearly complete.

Phase 06 — November 2024

501st Approved.

After months of sourcing, painting, weathering, and refining — the submission was made. And the approval came back. BH-7024 was officially a member of the Bounty Hunters Guild. A lifelong dream fulfilled. Boba Fett's legacy, carried forward.
