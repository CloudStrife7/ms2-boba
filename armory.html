Weathering & Patina

"Hosted an armor party this weekend and began using the bondo / acetone method to get the screen accurate look. Felt really good about this as it was a new technique to learn." The RedBowProps V3 helmet arrived pre-assembled, painted and weathered — "it's officially the coolest Star Wars item I own."

Part-01: Helmet

Moss green dome with burgundy mandibles. Features iconic dent, T-visor, rangefinder assembly, and eighteen orange kill-stripes.

Part-02: Chest Armor

Multi-plate dark green with sharp return edges. Left chest has red-backed slots, right displays Jaster Mereel's sigil.

Part-03: Gauntlets

Deep burgundy left and right shells. Left features calculator keypad and dual-laser housing. Right has whipcord housing and dart details.

Part-04: Shoulder Armor

Golden orange six-pointed design. Right shoulder has ovular dent. Left displays dark red Mythosaur skull on white background.

Part-05: Knee Armor

Golden orange with upside-down F detail and dart launcher pads. Right has one cylindrical launcher, left has two.

Part-06: Jet Pack

OT-style dark satin green with golden yellow step and collar. Features repaired damage patch with rivets and beacon assembly.

Part-07: Flak Jacket

Black plain weave cotton with crescent shoulder tabs and horizontal ribbing. Center back zipper with jet pack harness openings.

Part-08: Belt & Holster

Reddish-brown leather ammo belt with brass buckle and four pouches. Western-style drop-leg holster on right side.

Part-09: Gloves

Two-tone leather with black base and reddish-brown fingertips. Din Djarin-style flexible hand plates with forward-pointing triangle.

Part-10: Boots & Gaiters

Black distressed leather gaiters from knee to heel. Heavily weathered boots with five vertical knurled rows on toe box.

Part-11: Girth Belt

Three-strand braided polypropylene rope in black-brown-beige pattern. Secured with black vinyl at ends, closes with Velcro.

Part-12: Hakama Pants

Excessively baggy black raw silk with pleated top section. Dusty brown weathering on legs. Tattered sash hangs from right hip.
